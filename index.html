Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

VaCart ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application ("the App") handles user data.

Information Collection and Use

VaCart does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The app only fetches publicly available train vacancy information from the Indian Railway APIs.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any user data to third parties. All data displayed in the app comes directly from external railway APIs.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party APIs (e.g., Indian Railway API) to fetch live train vacancy data. These services may have their own privacy policies.

Security

Since no personal data is collected, there is no risk of personal data misuse. All communications with APIs are handled securely.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no personal information is collected, the app is safe for all age groups.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
